## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** Commonly called narrow oceanfront peninsula between Pacific Ocean and Mission Bay, centered on Mission Blvd boardwalk, Belmont Park, and Santa Clara Point; distinct from broader PB community plan area.
- **ZIP codes:** 92109 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** Pacific Beach guide covers Garnet-centric PB. Mission Beach is **boardwalk beach-town strip** with vacation-rental density, Belmont Park, and bay-ocean dual frontage—not PB residential mesa blocks.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: Beachfront condos and apartments, duplex/triplex, older SFR on narrow lots; strong STR/vacation rental presence.
- Notable subpockets: South Mission Beach near Belmont Park; north MB bordering PB; bay-side vs ocean-side blocks.
- Omissions / qualified language: STR regulations and HOA limits vary sharply—verify per unit; noise from boardwalk in oceanfront units.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Parks/beaches: Mission Beach boardwalk, Belmont Park, Mission Bay beaches, Crystal Pier (nearby PB).
- Commercial: Mission Blvd beach bars, Belmont Park amusement, mini-golf.
- Walkability / transit: High along boardwalk; MTS Route 8; seasonal crowds.
- Character: Tourist and party-adjacent beach town; active outdoor; not quiet family suburban.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major routes: Mission Blvd, I-5, I-8.
- Typical destinations: Downtown (~15 min), UTC (~20 min), airport (~15 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: Summer/weekend Mission Blvd gridlock; limited resident parking.

## 5. Schools

- School districts: San Diego Unified (verify; many MB addresses feed PB/Bay Park schools).
- FAQ pattern: "Research specific school boundaries before buying"
- **Do NOT** claim rankings without official source + date
