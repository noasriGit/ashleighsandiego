# Research Brief: Mission Bay (`mission-bay`)

**Tier:** 3 · **Parent:** pacific-beach · **Date:** 2026-06-28 · **Author:**

---

## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** Commonly called bay-front neighborhoods bordering Mission Bay Park—including Mission Beach bay side, Crown Point, Pacific Beach Mission Bay adjacency, and condo clusters on Quivira Way and Mariners Basin (buyer searches often mean bay-adjacent housing, not the park itself).
- **ZIP codes:** 92109 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** Pacific Beach guide emphasizes Garnet/beach culture. Mission Bay focus is **waterfront recreation and bay-view condos** with boating/kayak lifestyle—not PB bar scene or ocean boardwalk party strip.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: Bay-view condos and townhomes (1970s–2000s), some SFR near bay edges; marina-adjacent units on Quivira.
- Notable subpockets: Quivira Way condo complexes; Mariners Basin; bay-side Mission Beach blocks; Hilton area (commercial, not residential core).
- Omissions / qualified language: "Mission Bay" listings sometimes span Crown Point or MB—clarify bay-view vs oceanfront in copy.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Parks: Mission Bay Park (largest aquatic park in city), Ski Beach, Bonita Cove, playground and path network.
- Recreation: Kayak, paddleboard, sailing, SeaWorld San Diego (adjacent—commercial/tourism).
- Walkability / transit: Paths extensive; car for groceries; MTS Route 8/9.
- Character: Recreation-forward; mixed residents and visitors; family daytime, quieter nights than Garnet.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major routes: I-5, Mission Blvd, Sea World Dr, I-8.
- Typical destinations: Downtown (~15 min), airport (~12 min), UTC (~18 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: SeaWorld/I-5 interchange events affect traffic; summer bay parking competitive.

## 5. Schools

- School districts: San Diego Unified (verify by address; Crown Point/MB school zones).
- FAQ pattern: "Research specific school boundaries before buying"
- **Do NOT** claim rankings without official source + date
