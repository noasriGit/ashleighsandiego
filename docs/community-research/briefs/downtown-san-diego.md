# Research Brief: Downtown San Diego (`downtown-san-diego`)

**Tier:** 3 · **Parent:** hillcrest · **Date:** 2026-06-28 · **Author:**

---

## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** City Center / Downtown Community Plan area including Gaslamp Quarter, East Village, Columbia District, Marina District, and Cortez Hill—roughly bounded by San Diego Bay, I-5, and Balboa Park foothills.
- **ZIP codes:** 92101 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** Hillcrest guide covers Uptown walkable neighborhoods north of downtown. Downtown is **high-rise urban core** employment and entertainment center—condo/tower living, not bungalow residential Hillcrest.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: High-rise condos, loft conversions, some low-rise in Gaslamp; rental-heavy; limited SFR.
- Notable subpockets: East Village (Petco Park adjacency); Gaslamp historic loft district; Columbia waterfront towers; Cortez Hill residential edge.
- Omissions / qualified language: HOA and mello-roos vary by tower; verify building litigation and reserve studies.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Commercial: Gaslamp dining/nightlife, East Village breweries, Seaport Village / Embarcadero, Petco Park, San Diego Convention Center.
- Walkability / transit: Very high; full MTS trolley network (Blue/Orange/Green), Amtrak Santa Fe Depot.
- Character: Urban professional and entertainment; tourist/event traffic; 24/7 activity in Gaslamp; family buyers should weigh school/park access honestly.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major routes: I-5, I-8, Pacific Hwy; regional employment largely in-place.
- Typical destinations: Sorrento Valley (~25 min), UTC (~25 min), airport (~10 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: Convention and game-day traffic; parking costly for car owners.

## 5. Schools

- School districts: San Diego Unified (downtown enrollment limited—verify; City Prep or outside-neighborhood choices common).
- FAQ pattern: "Research specific school boundaries before buying"
- **Do NOT** claim rankings without official source + date
