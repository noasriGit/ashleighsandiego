## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** Distinct community planning area north of Mission Valley, bounded roughly by USD campus, SR-163, I-805, and Mission Valley rim; includes residential mesa and canyon pockets (Acorn Canyon, Tecolote Canyon edges).
- **ZIP codes:** 92111 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** Mission Valley guide covers commercial corridor and newer master-planned areas (Civita). Linda Vista is **diverse established residential mesa** with USD adjacency and canyon parks—not MV shopping/trolley lifestyle.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: Post-war single-family, multi-family apartments (including student-adjacent rental near USD), some townhomes.
- Notable subpockets: USD border streets; Linda Vista Multi-Cultural Canyon; areas near Coleman University site (redevelopment context).
- Omissions / qualified language: Student rental concentration near USD varies block-by-block—verify HOA/rental restrictions if relevant.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Parks: Linda Vista Multi-Cultural Canyon, Tecolote Canyon Natural Park, Montalvo Park.
- Institutions: University of San Diego (USD), Kaiser Permanente San Diego Medical Center.
- Walkability / transit: Limited; MTS routes on Linda Vista Rd and Morena Blvd; no trolley in-pocket.
- Character: Established multicultural mesa; university-adjacent; canyon open space; more affordable than Mission Valley new-build.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major freeways: SR-163, I-805, I-8 via Mission Valley.
- Typical destinations: Downtown (~12 min), USD (in-area), UTC (~15 min), airport (~12 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: Morena Blvd and SR-163 connectors congested at peaks.

## 5. Schools

- School districts: San Diego Unified (Linda Vista elementary schools—verify by address).
- FAQ pattern: "Research specific school boundaries before buying"
- **Do NOT** claim rankings without official source + date
