## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** Recognized community within Clairemont Mesa planning area and broader Kearny Mesa community plan; commercial and residential mix centered on Convoy Street, Kearny Villa Rd, and SR-163 corridor between Clairemont and San Diego State University approaches.
- **ZIP codes:** 92111 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** Clairemont guide emphasizes residential mesa and canyons. Kearny Mesa is **Convoy dining/auto-row commercial hub** with adjacent apartments and SFR pockets—buyer fit skews commute + diverse retail, not canyon-trail suburban.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: Apartments and condos near Convoy; 1960s–1980s SFR pockets on mesa blocks; some townhome infill.
- Notable subpockets: Convoy Street corridor (rental-heavy); residential streets north of Clairemont Mesa Blvd; areas near Montgomery Field.
- Omissions / qualified language: Convoy redevelopment plans evolving—verify current entitlements before citing specific projects.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Parks: Kearny Mesa Recreation Center, Miramar National Cemetery adjacency (cultural anchor), nearby Rose Canyon.
- Commercial: Convoy Street (Korean BBQ, Asian markets, auto dealers); Mesa College campus nearby.
- Walkability / transit: Convoy corridor walkable for dining; car-primary for errands; MTS Route 20/41 serve Convoy.
- Character: Diverse dining hub; working commercial mesa; less "neighborhood village" than North Clairemont.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major freeways: SR-163, I-805, I-8.
- Typical destinations: UTC (~12 min), downtown (~15 min), UCSD (~20 min), airport (~15 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: Convoy Street traffic heavy evenings/weekends.

## 5. Schools

- School districts: San Diego Unified (verify by address; Mesa College is community college, not K-12).
- FAQ pattern: "Research specific school boundaries before buying"
- **Do NOT** claim rankings without official source + date
