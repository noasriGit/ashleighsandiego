## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** Redevelopment-focused area within Midway-Pacific Highway Community Plan, roughly bounded by I-5, I-8, Lytton St, and Sports Arena Blvd (Pechanga Arena)—includes former sports arena site and commercial corridors approaching SAN airport.
- **ZIP codes:** 92110 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** Point Loma guide covers peninsula neighborhoods and Liberty Station. Midway District is **airport-adjacent redevelopment zone** with industrial/commercial legacy and emerging residential infill—not PL coastal or established SFR hills.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: Older apartments and condos; new mixed-use infill planned/proposed around arena site; limited traditional SFR.
- Notable subpockets: Arena site redevelopment context; Rosecrans corridor; Lytton St commercial edges.
- Omissions / qualified language: Arena district plans evolve—verify current approved projects on city planning portal before citing specifics.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Commercial: Pechanga Arena San Diego (events), Rosecrans St retail, proximity to Liberty Station and Old Town.
- Parks: Nearby Rose Canyon, Dewey Elementary pocket parks; not park-rich in-core.
- Walkability / transit: Car-primary today; Old Town trolley ~1–2 mi; future transit depends on redevelopment.
- Character: Redevelopment transitional; event traffic; airport noise disclosure possible—verify FAA contours per address.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major routes: I-5, I-8, Rosecrans St, Pacific Hwy.
- Typical destinations: Downtown (~10 min), airport (~5 min), Liberty Station (~5 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: Event nights at arena; I-5/I-8 interchange chronic congestion.

## 5. Schools

- School districts: San Diego Unified (verify; often Point Loma or Dewey cluster).
