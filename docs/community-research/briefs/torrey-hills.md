## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** Commonly called residential pocket within the Carmel Valley Community Planning Area; not a separate City Community Plan subarea. Boundaries generally follow Carmel Valley Rd corridor east of I-5, south of Carmel Country Rd, and north of Torrey Pines State Reserve approaches.
- **ZIP codes:** 92130 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** Carmel Valley guide covers the full master-planned valley (Pacific Highlands Ranch, CV proper, east CV). Torrey Hills is the **western, canyon-adjacent family pocket** closer to Torrey Pines bluffs and I-5/56 interchange—not the UTC retail core or newer east-side tracts.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: Single-family detached (1980s–2000s tract), some townhomes near canyon edges; limited condo inventory vs central Carmel Valley.
- Notable subpockets: Streets backing Los Peñasquitos Canyon Preserve; pockets near Torrey Hills Community Park.
- Omissions / qualified language: Exact tract vintage and HOA counts vary by pocket; do not cite median price without MLS snapshot date.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Parks: Torrey Hills Neighborhood Park, Los Peñasquitos Canyon Preserve trail access, Carmel Valley Community Park (nearby).
- Commercial: Carmel Valley Rd retail (Whole Foods, restaurants); Westfield UTC roughly 10–15 min drive.
- Walkability / transit: Car-oriented; MTS Route 101/972 serve Carmel Valley Rd corridor (qualitative—check sdmts.com for current schedules).
- Character: Family-oriented suburban; quieter than UTC; canyon trails and Torrey Pines proximity define outdoor lifestyle.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major freeways: I-5, SR-56 (Ted Williams Pkwy), I-805 via 56.
- Typical destinations: UCSD/Sorrento Valley biotech (~15–25 min off-peak), UTC (~10 min), downtown (~25–35 min), SAN airport (~25 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: SR-56 and I-5 merge congestion during AM/PM peaks.

## 5. Schools

- School districts: San Diego Unified School District (address-specific; verify at sandiegounified.org boundaries).
- Notable attendance pattern: Many addresses feed Carmel Valley / Torrey Pines cluster schools; boundaries shift—confirm before offer.
- FAQ pattern: "Research specific school boundaries before buying"
- **Do NOT** claim rankings without official source + date
