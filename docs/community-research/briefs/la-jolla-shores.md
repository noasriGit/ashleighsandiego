## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** Commonly called beach neighborhood north of La Jolla village along La Jolla Shores Drive and Camino del Oro, adjacent to La Jolla Shores beach and Scripps Institution of Oceanography.
- **ZIP codes:** 92037 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** La Jolla guide covers peninsula-wide market. Shores is **family-friendly flat beach neighborhood** with calm surf, kayak culture, and Scripps adjacency—not village cliff tourism or mesa golf course areas.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: Single-family beach cottages and larger remodels; some condos on Avenida de la Playa; multi-generational family homes.
- Notable subpockets: Beach-block streets south of Shores beach; areas near Kellogg Park; Scripps campus border.
- Omissions / qualified language: Beach-block premium varies widely; flood/coastal zone disclosures may apply—verify per parcel.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Parks/beaches: La Jolla Shores Beach, Kellogg Park, Scripps Pier viewpoint.
- Institutions: Scripps Institution of Oceanography (UCSD), Birch Aquarium (nearby).
- Walkability / transit: Flat walk/bike to beach; MTS Route 30; village uphill ~1 mi.
- Character: Family beach town within La Jolla; surf/kayak/outdoor; quieter evenings than Cove.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major routes: La Jolla Shores Dr, Torrey Pines Rd, I-5.
- Typical destinations: UCSD (~10 min), UTC (~12 min), Sorrento Valley (~15 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: Summer beach traffic on La Jolla Shores Dr.
