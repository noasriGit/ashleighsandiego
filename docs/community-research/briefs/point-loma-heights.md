## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** Commonly called elevated residential area on the Point Loma peninsula above Sunset Cliffs and Roseville-Fleetridge, including hills along Chatsworth Blvd, Canon St, and Point Loma Ave with ocean and bay views.
- **ZIP codes:** 92106 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** Point Loma guide spans peninsula including Liberty Station and Loma Portal. Point Loma Heights is **view hillside SFR enclave** above coastal cliffs—not waterfront Loma Portal or Liberty Station urban village.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: Mid-century and contemporary single-family on sloped lots; view premiums on west-facing streets; limited condos.
- Notable subpockets: Chatsworth Blvd ridge; areas near Point Loma Nazarene University (PLNU); Canon St view corridors.
- Omissions / qualified language: Hillside geologic reports common; verify slope stability and view easements per property.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Parks: Sunset Cliffs Natural Park (adjacent below), Point Loma Native Plant Garden, Cabrillo National Monument (~15 min drive).
- Institutions: Point Loma Nazarene University (PLNU).
- Walkability / transit: Low hills; car primary; MTS Route 28 on Point Loma Ave.
- Character: Quiet residential views; college-adjacent; sunset cliff access nearby but not flat walk-to-beach.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major routes: Chatsworth Blvd, Catalina Blvd, I-5, I-8 via Rosecrans.
- Typical destinations: Downtown (~15 min), airport (~12 min), Liberty Station (~10 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: Narrow hillside streets; Catalina Blvd school/college traffic.

## 5. Schools

- School districts: San Diego Unified (Point Loma HS cluster for many addresses—verify).
- FAQ pattern: "Research specific school boundaries before buying"
- **Do NOT** claim rankings without official source + date
