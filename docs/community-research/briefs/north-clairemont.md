## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** Commonly called northern portion of Clairemont Mesa within the Clairemont Mesa Community Planning Area; generally north of Clairemont Mesa Blvd and west/east mesa neighborhoods with canyon adjacency (Marian Bear, San Clemente canyons).
- **ZIP codes:** 92117 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** Clairemont guide spans full mesa (south Clairemont, Balboa Ave corridor). North Clairemont is **quieter residential mesa + canyon-edge** with stronger family-SFR profile and Tecolote Canyon access—not the Balboa commercial strip focus.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: Post-war and mid-century single-family, some 1970s tracts; ranch-style common on mesa lots.
- Notable subpockets: Streets bordering Marian Bear Memorial Park; North Clairemont Rec Center area; pockets near Clairemont Mesa Blvd east.
- Omissions / qualified language: Housing age varies widely block-to-block; avoid uniform "mid-century" claim without address verification.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Parks: Marian Bear Memorial Park (San Clemente Canyon), North Clairemont Community Park, Tecolote Canyon Natural Park (nearby).
- Commercial: Clairemont Mesa Blvd retail; Convoy Street Asian dining district (~10 min).
- Walkability / transit: Moderate in pockets; MTS Routes 25/27 serve mesa corridors.
- Character: Established suburban mesa; canyon hiking; more affordable than coastal SD—family and commute-oriented.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major freeways: I-805, I-5 via Balboa Ave, SR-52.
- Typical destinations: UTC (~15 min), downtown (~15–20 min), Mission Valley (~10 min), beach (~15 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: SR-52 and I-805 interchanges congested at peaks.

## 5. Schools

- School districts: San Diego Unified (Clairemont cluster schools—verify by address).
- FAQ pattern: "Research specific school boundaries before buying"
- **Do NOT** claim rankings without official source + date
