## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** Commonly called area spanning coastal bluffs and mesa neighborhoods north of UCSD, overlapping La Jolla Community Planning Area and Torrey Pines State Natural Reserve environs; includes residential tracts near Torrey Pines Golf Course and north La Jolla mesa.
- **ZIP codes:** 92037 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** La Jolla guide covers village, beaches, and full prestige market. Torrey Pines emphasizes **mesa/coastal-bluff living, golf/reserve adjacency, and UCSD proximity**—not village retail or Cove tourism.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: Single-family on mesa (1960s–1990s), custom view homes on bluffs; some condos near UCSD/SCRIPPS; limited inventory vs La Jolla village.
- Notable subpockets: Country Club area near Torrey Pines Golf Course; north mesa tracts; bluff streets with reserve views.
- Omissions / qualified language: "Torrey Pines" also names the state reserve—clarify residential vs park in copy.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Parks: Torrey Pines State Natural Reserve, Torrey Pines Gliderport, Pottery Canyon (nearby).
- Institutions: UCSD campus, Scripps Institution of Oceanography, Torrey Pines Golf Course (public).
- Walkability / transit: Car-primary; MTS UC San Diego Blue Line serves campus area; bluff trails for recreation not errands.
- Character: Coastal/outdoor, academic-adjacent, quieter than La Jolla village; reserve and ocean views define identity.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major freeways: I-5, Genesee Ave, Torrey Pines Rd.
- Typical destinations: UCSD (in-area), UTC (~10 min), Sorrento Valley (~10–15 min), downtown (~25 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: Torrey Pines Rd and I-5 merge points congested; gliderport events can affect parking seasonally.

## 5. Schools

- School districts: San Diego Unified (La Jolla cluster schools for many addresses—verify per address).
- FAQ pattern: "Research specific school boundaries before buying"
- **Do NOT** claim rankings without official source + date
