# Research Brief: Civita (`civita`)

**Tier:** 2 · **Parent:** mission-valley · **Date:** 2026-06-28 · **Author:**

## 1. Geography & boundaries

- **Official definition:** Master-planned community within Mission Valley East subarea; developed on former quarry site bounded roughly by Friars Rd, SR-163, and Mission Valley. Recognized as distinct neighborhood by City planning and developer (Sudberry Properties).
- **ZIP codes:** 92108 (cross-check `src/data/community-zips.ts`)
- **Relationship to parent:** Mission Valley guide covers retail corridor (Fashion Valley, stadium area), older apartments, and freeway-oriented pockets. Civita is **new-build master-planned residential** with parks, townhomes/SFRs, and internal walkability—not the commercial MV core.

## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: Newer townhomes, detached SFRs, some condos/apartments in multi-phase development (2010s–2020s+).
- Notable subpockets: Civita central park area; phases along Via Alta and Franklin Ridge; rental apartments on periphery.
- Omissions / qualified language: Active development may continue; amenity completion phases vary—verify current phase maps with developer site.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Parks: Civita Park, canyon open space, community pools/clubhouse (HOA-governed).
- Commercial: Civita Town Center (retail/dining); Fashion Valley and Mission Valley centers nearby.
- Walkability / transit: Internal walkable core; MTS Green Line trolley at Mission Valley Center/Fashion Valley (~1–2 mi); bus routes on Friars Rd.
- Character: Family-oriented master-planned; modern vs legacy Mission Valley apartments; freeway-visible but buffered by design.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major freeways: I-8, I-805, SR-163, I-5 via Friars Rd.
- Typical destinations: Downtown (~15 min), airport (~15 min), UTC (~20 min), SDSU (~10 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: Friars Rd and SR-163 interchange is peak bottleneck; trolley access improves vs car-only MV pockets.

## 5. Schools

- School districts: San Diego Unified (verify by address; some areas feed Madison/Clairemont cluster).
- FAQ pattern: "Research specific school boundaries before buying"
- **Do NOT** claim rankings without official source + date
