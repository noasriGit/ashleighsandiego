## 2. Housing stock

- Dominant types: 1950s–1970s single-family tracts (post-Miramar development), some townhomes; limited high-rise inventory.
- Notable subpockets: Tierrasanta border streets; areas near Sharp Memorial Hospital; Ruffin Canyon adjacency.
- Omissions / qualified language: Aircraft noise disclosure possible near Miramar flight paths—buyers should verify FAA/community noise contours for specific addresses.

## 3. Lifestyle & anchors

- Parks: Ruffin Canyon Open Space, Serra Mesa Community Park, nearby Mission Trails Regional Park access.
- Institutions: Sharp Memorial Hospital, Sharp Rees-Stealy medical offices.
- Walkability / transit: Car-primary; MTS routes on Aero Dr and Mission Center Rd; no trolley in-pocket.
- Character: Quiet suburban mesa; medical-employment adjacency; central SD location without MV commercial intensity.

## 4. Commute & connectivity

- Major freeways: SR-163, I-805, I-8, I-15 via 163.
- Typical destinations: Downtown (~15 min), SDSU (~10 min), UTC (~15 min), airport (~15 min)—roughly, depends on time of day.
- Qualifiers: SR-163 through Serra Mesa is major commuter artery; peak delays common.
